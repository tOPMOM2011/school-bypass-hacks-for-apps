How to get linewize or classwise off

Before you start find were linewize or classwise is located on your computer it is normally in program files x86 this will help later on

1 step: open settings

2 step: type reset this pc (don't worry this won't reset the pc)

3 step: press advanced startup

4 step: go to troubleshoot

5 step: command prompt

6 step: in cmd type notepad

7 step: in notepad press file on the top left

8 step: press open

9 step: like i said at the start go to were you found linewize or classwize

10 step: right click and delete it

11 step: get out of notepad and type exit in cmd

12 step: press continue and your done
